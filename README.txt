Connect your WooCommerce store with the invoicing platform SmartPablo

== Description ==

SmartPablo is money management platform for ecommerce and small business owners. 
Automates the entire sales cycle across all of your sales channels, including your WooCommerce store. More at [SmartPablo.com](https://www.smartpablo.com/).

This extension handles the orders sync between your store and SmartPablo.

Main features of the SmartPablo WooCommerce extension include:

* Automatically sends orders to the [SmartPablo](https://www.smartpablo.com) app.
* Invoice or Order is automatically created on the background
* Add fields for invoice details to WooCommerce Checkout form.
* Custom invoice numbering.
* Invoice design that match your brand (color, logo, fields)

Note: your orders data will be transmitted to [SmartPablo](https://www.smartpablo.com) app via HTTP requests to endpoint connect.SmartPablo.com. Connection is protected by industry standard SSL certificate.


== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire SmartPablo folder *smartpablo-woocommerce* to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory (or use WordPress native installer in Plugins > Add New Plugin). And activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
2. Open extensions settings
3. Sign into your SmartPablo account or create a new account for free
4. Allow access to WooCommerce plugin to your SmartPablo account
